## Overview

Llama 4 Scout is Meta's groundbreaking open-weight natively multimodal model, released as part of the Llama 4 family. It represents the first open-source multimodal LLM with MoE architecture and unprecedented context length.

## Architecture & Scale

- **Active Parameters**: 17 billion
- **Total Parameters**: 109 billion
- **Expert Configuration**: 16 experts (MoE)
- **Context Window**: 10 million tokens (unprecedented)
- **Modality**: Native text and image understanding
- **Pre-training**: 40 trillion tokens of multimodal data

## Training Data

- Multimodal mix of text and image data
- Publicly available content
- Licensed data
- Meta product and service information
- Massive scale for frontier capabilities

## Performance Benchmarks

- **Coding**: Exceeds Llama 3.1 405B
- **Reasoning**: Superior performance
- **Long Context**: Handles 10M token documents
- **Image Understanding**: Strong visual reasoning
- **Overall**: Competitive with frontier models

## Advantages
