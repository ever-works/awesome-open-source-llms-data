## Overview

Jamba 1.5 is AI21 Labs' latest advancement in efficient language models, featuring a revolutionary hybrid SSM-Transformer architecture. Designed for enterprise applications requiring long-context understanding and maximum efficiency.

## Architecture Innovation

- **Hybrid Design**: Combines Mamba SSM with Transformer layers
- **MoE Layers**: Mixture-of-Experts for selective activation
- **Total Parameters**: 52 billion
- **Active Parameters**: Only 12 billion per forward pass
- **Efficiency**: MoE enables dramatic activation reduction

## Context & Performance

- Context Window: 256,000 tokens (handles lengthy documents)
- Throughput: 3x improvement on long contexts
- Quality: Maintains top performance across tasks
- Speed: Fastest processing on market for long sequences
