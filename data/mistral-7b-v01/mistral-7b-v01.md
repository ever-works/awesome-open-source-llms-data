## Overview

The Mistral-7B-v0.1 is a pretrained generative text model with 7 billion parameters.

## Features

- Grouped-Query Attention
- Sliding-Window Attention
- Byte-fallback BPE tokenizer

## Performance

- Outperforms Llama 2 13B on all benchmarks tested.

## Availability

The model is available for free and open source use on the Hugging Face Hub.
