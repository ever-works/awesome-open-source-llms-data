## Overview

OLMo 3 is the Allen Institute for AI's (AI2) latest family of fully open language models, representing the cutting edge of transparent, open AI research. Unlike many "open weight" models, OLMo provides complete access to training data, code, and methodology, making it truly open for research and development.

## Architecture

- **Model Sizes:** 7B and 32B parameters
- **Model Type:** Advanced Transformer architecture
- **Design:** Optimized for reasoning and general capabilities
- **Training:** Fully documented and reproducible process

## Key Features

- Complete transparency: training data, code, and weights all public
- Best fully-open 32B base model available
- Advanced reasoning capabilities
- Reproducible training process
- Strong general-purpose performance
- Built for scientific research and development
- No restrictions on data access

## What Makes OLMo Truly Open

### Complete Transparency
1. **Training Data:** Full access to training datasets
2. **Training Code:** Complete training infrastructure available
3. **Model Weights:** All model parameters publicly accessible
4. **Methodology:** Detailed documentation of training process
5. **Evaluation:** Comprehensive evaluation framework

### Contrast with "Open Weight" Models
- Many models only release weights, not training data
- OLMo provides everything needed to reproduce and understand the model
- Enables genuine scientific research and auditing
