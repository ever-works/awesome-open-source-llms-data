## Overview

EXAONE 4.0 32B is LG's 32B parameter open source language model, noted for strong reasoning capabilities. It sits in the medium-scale tier for self-hosting, offering a sweet spot between quality and compute cost.

## Benchmarks & Performance

- Category: Reasoning-focused model
- Positioned in the VRAM bracket of 24-48GB, suitable for A100/H100 or multi-GPU consumer setups

## Deployment

- **VRAM Requirements**: 24-48GB
- **Hardware**: Requires A100/H100 or multi-GPU consumer setup
- **Frameworks**: Compatible with Ollama, vLLM, llama.cpp, and Text Generation Inference
- **Quantization**: Supports GGUF quantization to reduce memory needs by 50-75% with approximately 2% quality loss

## Licensing

Released as an open source model, free to self-host and fine-tune for commercial use.
