## Overview
GLM-4.7 (Thinking) is an open-source large language model developed by Z AI, known for its robust performance in thinking and coding tasks. It secured a top spot in February 2026 rankings.

## Key Features
- **Cognitive Capabilities:** Achieves a Quality Index of 41.78, reflecting strong cognitive and reasoning abilities.
- **Coding Proficiency:** Scores 89% on LiveCodeBench, making it a highly effective model for code generation and analysis.
- **Extensive Context:** Supports a 200K token context window, enabling it to handle complex and lengthy inputs.
