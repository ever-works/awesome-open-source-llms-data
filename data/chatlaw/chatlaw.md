## Overview

ChatLaw is a domain-specific open-source language model fine-tuned for legal assistance tasks. Developed by Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications and collaborating institutions, it provides specialized support for the Chinese legal system.

## Capabilities

- Legal consultation and Q&A
- Chinese legal document analysis
- Case law reference and reasoning
- Legal document drafting assistance
- Multi-round legal dialogue

## Architecture

- Fine-tuned variant based on existing open-source LLMs
- Domain corpus: Chinese legal texts, judgments, regulations, and legal literature
- Released with open weights

## Use Cases

- Legal professionals seeking reference information
- Law students and researchers
- Public legal education and consultation
- Law firm automation and document review

## Deployment

- Open weights available for download
- Can be deployed on local infrastructure for privacy-sensitive legal applications
