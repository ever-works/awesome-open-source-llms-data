## Overview

Open Reasoning Tasks is a comprehensive open-source repository maintained by Nous Research, providing standardized reasoning tasks and benchmarks for evaluating language models.

## Purpose

- Benchmark reasoning capabilities
- Train reasoning models
- Evaluate model performance
- Share methodology
- Advance reasoning research

## Components

- Diverse reasoning task collection
- Standardized evaluation metrics
- Training methodology
- Reproducible benchmarks
- Community contributions

## Methodology

- Solving phase: Workers attempt problems
- Self-assessment: Evaluate own solutions
- Finalization: Choose best submission
- Standardized metrics: Consistent evaluation

## Use Cases

- Model development
- Reasoning research
- Benchmark evaluation
- Task creation
- Methodology study
