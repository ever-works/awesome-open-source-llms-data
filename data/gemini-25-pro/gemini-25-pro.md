## Overview

Gemini 2.5 Pro was released by Google on March 26, 2025. It represents Google's most significant advancement in the AI race at the time of release, with a focus on complex reasoning and layered problem-solving.

## Architecture

- **Thinking model foundation**: Uses reinforcement learning and chain-of-thought (CoT) prompting built into the core architecture
- **Reasoning architecture**: Analyzes context patterns and draws logical conclusions when predicting outputs
- **Early fusion**: Blends vision and language data during training from the ground up

## Features

- **Advanced reasoning**: Breaks down complex problems, analyzing both the big picture and fine details
- **Real-time web browsing**: Can access and incorporate live internet data
- **Multimodal understanding**: Processes text, images, audio, and video simultaneously
- **Enhanced coding capability**: Significant improvement over Gemini 2.0 and 1.5 for coding tasks

## Benchmark Performance

- **#1 on LMArena leaderboard**: Rankings based on real human preference, not just benchmarks
- **GPQA**: Top scores in graduate-level science tasks
- **AIME 2025**: Top performance in advanced math competitions
- **Humanity's Last Exam**: 18.8% score without shortcuts like majority voting

## Use Cases

- Complex, layered problem-solving
- Mathematics and science tasks
- Coding applications
- Multimodal content analysis

## Limitations

- Still in experimental release phase
- Proprietary model — not available for open modification
- API or Google platform access only
