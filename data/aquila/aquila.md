## Overview

Aquila is an open-source large language model series developed by the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI), a nonprofit research institution. Part of the WuDao project, Aquila represents China's contribution to open-source AI research.

## Model Variants

### Aquila-7B
- **Parameters**: 7 billion
- **Focus**: Code generation
- **Availability**: Published on Hugging Face
- **Use Case**: Programming assistance

### AquilaChat-7B
- **Parameters**: 7 billion
- **Focus**: Conversational AI
- **Availability**: Published on Hugging Face
- **Use Case**: Dialogue and chat applications
