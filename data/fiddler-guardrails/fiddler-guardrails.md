## Overview

Fiddler Guardrails is part of Fiddler's enterprise AI reliability platform, providing real-time input and output filtering with support for organization-specific policies, risk threshold tuning, and comprehensive analytics for continuous governance.

## Features

- **Custom policy guardrails**: Define bespoke rules beyond generic safety categories to match organization-specific requirements
- **Configurable risk thresholds**: Set threshold scores for each risk dimension independently to tune acceptance/rejection criteria
- **Optimized latency**: Purpose-built safety models execute in under 100ms per request
- **Integrated monitoring**: Analyzes risky prompts and responses over time, providing root-cause analysis for AI risk teams
- **Detailed explanations**: Provides detailed reasons when content is blocked, including which specific policy was violated
- **Real-time blocking**: Intercepts and blocks non-compliant content during inference
- **Air-gapped deployment**: Can be deployed in fully isolated environments with all data staying local
- **Pattern analysis**: Identifies systemic issues such as recurring false positives or persistent evasion attempts

## Use Cases

- Enterprise AI governance requiring detailed audit trails
- Multi-application safety monitoring across an AI portfolio
- Highly regulated industries needing local data processing
