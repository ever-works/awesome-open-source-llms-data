## Overview

Allganize provides an on-premises AI governance and safety layer for enterprise deployments, emphasizing comprehensive monitoring, audit trail creation, and risk mitigation for LLM-powered applications.

## Features

- **Kill-switch mechanisms**: Ability to halt AI outputs and mitigate risks from unexpected behavior
- **Robust monitoring and logging**: Comprehensive audit trails for compliance and tracking AI agent behavior
- **On-premises deployment**: All data and processing stays within the organization's environment
- **Governance layer**: Centralized control for AI policies, risk management, and compliance
- **Risk mitigation framework**: Systematic approach to identifying and addressing AI safety issues

## Use Cases

- Enterprise AI compliance requiring detailed audit trails
- Regulated industries needing kill-switch capabilities
- Organizations deploying AI agents requiring behavior monitoring
