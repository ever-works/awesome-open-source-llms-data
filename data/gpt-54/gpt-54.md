## Overview

GPT-5.4 is OpenAI's latest flagship model, designed to be a comprehensive AI solution by merging the functionalities of previous models like GPT-4o, o3, and Codex. It is both multimodal, meaning it can process and generate various types of data beyond text, and capable of advanced reasoning.

## Features

- **Multimodal Capabilities:** Handles diverse data inputs and outputs.
- **Reasoning Model:** Breaks down complex problems into steps, reassesses issues, and approaches problems from different angles.
- **General Purpose:** Suitable for a wide range of tasks including text generation, summarization, and coding.
- **API Access:** Available for developers to integrate into their applications.
- **Adjustable Effort:** Through its API, users can set the effort the model expends on difficult problems.
- **Multiple Versions:** Offers full, nano, and mini versions to suit different computational needs.

## Access

GPT-5.4 is accessible through ChatGPT and as an API for developers. The API allows for detailed configuration, including setting the model's effort for complex tasks and choosing between different model sizes.
