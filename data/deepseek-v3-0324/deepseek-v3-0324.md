## Overview

DeepSeek-V3-0324 is an upgraded version of DeepSeek V3, released on March 24, 2025 by the Chinese company DeepSeek. It is open-source under the MIT license and competes directly with leading proprietary models like GPT-4.5 and Claude 3.7 Sonnet.

## Architecture

- **Mixture-of-Experts (MoE)**: 685 billion total parameters with only 37 billion activated per token
- **Efficient design**: High performance without excessive compute requirements

## Training Efficiency

- **GPU hours**: 2.788 million hours on NVIDIA H800 GPUs
- **Training cost**: Approximately $5.5 million — significantly less than major proprietary model training budgets

## Features

- **Open-source**: MIT license allows full access modification and redistribution
- **Math and coding excellence**: Competitive with GPT-4.5 and Claude 3.7 on complex tasks
- **Weight availability**: Model weights available on Hugging Face

## Benchmark Performance

- Strong performance on mathematics benchmarks
- Strong performance on coding benchmarks
- Competes head-to-head with GPT-4.5 and Claude 3.7 Sonnet on complex reasoning tasks

## Use Cases

- Academic and research applications
- Cost-effective deployment with open-source licensing
- Fine-tuning for domain-specific tasks
- Mathematics and coding applications
