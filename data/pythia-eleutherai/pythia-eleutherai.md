## Overview

Pythia is EleutherAI's suite of language models designed specifically to enable scientific research on large language models. Unlike production-focused models, Pythia prioritizes interpretability and scientific analysis by providing unprecedented access to training checkpoints and learning dynamics.

## Architecture

- **Model Sizes:** Multiple variants from small to large
- **Model Type:** Autoregressive Transformer
- **Training Checkpoints:** 154 checkpoints saved throughout training
- **Design Focus:** Scientific research and interpretability

## Key Features

### Unique Research Capabilities
- **154 Training Checkpoints:** Track model evolution during training

- **Interpretability Focus:** Designed for mechanistic interpretability research
