## Overview
GLM 4.6 is an open-source large language model specifically designed to manage and process long documents and extended interactive sessions without losing contextual coherence. It is a reliable tool for research teams and individuals who frequently work with verbose content.

## Capabilities
- **Long Text Processing**: Handles large volumes of text smoothly, maintaining coherence across different sections and retaining context from earlier parts of the document.
- **Extended Session Consistency**: Performs consistently during long operations or large-scale projects, preventing degradation of quality as sessions extend.
- **Research and Analysis**: Particularly effective for processing research papers, detailed reports, and in-depth analyses that may span many dozens of pages.

## Use Cases
- Academic and scientific research requiring analysis of extensive literature.
- Processing and summarizing lengthy reports and detailed documents.
- Applications that involve prolonged conversational or analytical sessions.
