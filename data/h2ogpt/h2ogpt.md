## Overview
h2oGPT provides complete transparency with an open license, making it particularly suitable for private deployments. It is often chosen by teams that handle sensitive data and require robust internal systems with full control over their information.

## Capabilities
- **Open License**: Features a fully open license with no usage restrictions, offering users complete control over deployment and modifications.
- **On-premise Deployment**: Can be run entirely on your own servers, ensuring complete data control and helping meet stringent security and compliance requirements.
- **Easy Fine-tuning**: Offers straightforward processes for fine-tuning with your own datasets, which does not require deep technical expertise.

## Use Cases
- Internal systems for organizations with strict data privacy needs.
- Applications requiring full control over the LLM's deployment and modifications.
- Customization with proprietary datasets without significant technical overhead.

## Hardware Considerations
- Requires low to mid-range hardware, making it accessible for various setups while ensuring data control.
