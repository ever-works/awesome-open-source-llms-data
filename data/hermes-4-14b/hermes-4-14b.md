## Overview

Hermes 4 14B brings the hybrid reasoning capabilities of the Hermes 4 family to a compact size suitable for resource-constrained environments. Delivers frontier-level performance without massive parameter overhead.

## Model Architecture

- **Parameters**: 14 billion
- **Base**: Llama 3.1 14B
- **Enhancement**: Hybrid reasoning training
- **Architecture**: Transformer with advanced tuning

## Hybrid Reasoning

- **Toggle Mechanism**: System prompt-based switching
- **Thinking Mode**: Extended chain of thought
- **Fast Mode**: Rapid intuitive responses
- **Flexibility**: Choose reasoning depth per task
