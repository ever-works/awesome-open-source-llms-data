## Overview
Falcon is an open-source large language model known for its ability to operate efficiently on limited hardware and function without an internet connection. This makes it a practical choice for environments requiring high security, such as air-gapped systems, or for locations where internet access is unreliable or non-existent.

## Capabilities
- **Hardware Efficiency**: Performs well on mid-range or basic server setups, making it accessible without significant infrastructure investments.
- **Offline Operation**: Can function entirely without internet connectivity, which is crucial for air-gapped environments or areas with unreliable network access.
- **Reliable for Routine Tasks**: Handles routine business tasks and straightforward applications reliably without adding unnecessary complexity to simple workflows.

## Use Cases
- Secure environments where internet access is prohibited or restricted.
- Remote locations with limited or no internet connectivity.
- Performing routine business operations and simple application tasks.
