## Overview

Hermes 4 is Nous Research's latest line of frontier-level open-source models released August 26, 2025. The 70B variant represents an aggressive push to rival commercial AI systems while maintaining open accessibility and user control.

## Model Family

Available in three sizes based on Llama 3.1:
- **14B**: Lightweight variant
- **70B**: Balanced performance/efficiency
- **405B**: Maximum capability

## Hybrid Reasoning Innovation

- **Toggle-On Reasoning**: Switch reasoning on/off dynamically
- **Explicit Thinking**: Use `<think>...</think>` tags for complex problems
- **Standard Mode**: Fast responses for straightforward tasks
- **Flexible Approach**: Choose reasoning depth per query

## Training Infrastructure

**Novel Systems:**
- **DataForge**: Graph-based synthetic data generator
- **Atropos**: Open-source reinforcement learning framework
- Both systems fully reproducible and available

**Training Scale:**
- Previous: 1M samples, 1.2B tokens
- Current: ~5M samples, ~60B tokens
- Blended reasoning and non-reasoning data
- Massive post-training corpus

## Performance

- Frontier-level performance in its weight class
- Surpasses ChatGPT on multiple benchmarks
- Competitive with Claude models
- Hybrid reasoning enables versatility
- Strong instruction following

## Philosophy

Nous Research emphasizes:
- User control over AI behavior
- Steerability without rigid constraints
- Transparency in training
- Freedom from corporate content policies
- Reproducible research
