## Overview

Gemma 3 is Google's third-generation family of lightweight, state-of-the-art open models built from the same technology that powers the Gemini family of large language models. Gemma models are considered "lightweight" versions of Gemini, optimized for efficiency while maintaining high performance.

## Architecture

- **Model Sizes:** 2B, 7B, 9B, 12B, and 27B parameters
- **Base Technology:** Same foundation as Gemini models
- **Design:** Optimized for low-latency, safe applications
- **Training:** Advanced techniques from Google's research

## Key Features

- State-of-the-art performance at smaller sizes
- Efficient inference for resource-constrained scenarios
- Built-in safety features and alignment
- Strong multilingual capabilities
- Optimized for both research and production
- Compatible with standard frameworks

## Performance Highlights

### Gemma-3-27B
- **Beats Gemini 1.5-Pro** across multiple benchmarks
- Exceptional performance for its size class
- Strong general knowledge and reasoning
- Excellent coding capabilities

### Size-Specific Strengths
- **2B:** Ultra-efficient for edge deployment
- **7B:** Best balance of performance and efficiency
- **9B:** Enhanced capabilities for specialized tasks
- **12B:** Strong general-purpose performance (by size tier)
- **27B:** Flagship model with top-tier results

### Benchmark Results
- Exceptional results across all size categories
- Outperforms larger open models in many tasks
- Competitive with much larger proprietary models
- Strong MMLU, coding, and reasoning scores

## Safety and Alignment

- Responsible AI considerations
