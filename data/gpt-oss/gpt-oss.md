## Overview

gpt-oss represents OpenAI's venture into open large language models, specifically focusing on reasoning capabilities. These models are available in two primary sizes: gpt-oss-20b (21 billion parameters) and gpt-oss-120b (117 billion parameters, utilizing a mixture-of-experts architecture). They are developed using the same advanced training techniques as OpenAI's proprietary models like o3 and GPT-4o.

## Features

- **Open Availability:** Can be downloaded, fine-tuned, and used for various purposes.
- **Reasoning Capabilities:** Designed to excel in complex problem-solving by breaking down tasks into manageable steps.
- **Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) Architecture:** The 120b version utilizes MoE for enhanced performance and efficiency.
- **State-of-the-Art Training:** Benefits from the latest advancements in OpenAI's model development.

## Access

These models are openly accessible, allowing developers and researchers to integrate them into their own applications and conduct further research. OpenAI has implemented measures to mitigate potential misuse.

## Pricing

As open models, gpt-oss is generally free to use, though deployment and operational costs for running the models on personal infrastructure would apply.
