## Overview

GPT-4.5 (codenamed Orion) was released by OpenAI on February 27, 2025 as a significant upgrade over earlier GPT-4 iterations.

## Features

- **128,000-token context window**: Enables long-term memory across extended conversations, maintaining consistency and context awareness
- **Improved directness**: Delivers more concise, to-the-point responses compared to previous versions, reducing unnecessary verbosity
- **Broader knowledge base**: Reduces hallucinations when discussing a wide range of topics
- **Natural conversational style**: More human-like interaction quality; optimized for casual chats and quick content generation
- **85.1% MMLU score**: Strong performance on major benchmarks for general knowledge and reasoning

## Performance

- MMLU: 85.1%
- 128K token memory window
- Optimized for speed and directness over deep technical problem-solving

## Use Cases

- Casual conversation and quick content generation
- Broad-knowledge queries across diverse topics
- Situations requiring concise, direct answers

## Limitations

- Not always the best choice for deep technical problem-solving
- Proprietary — cannot be fine-tuned or modified by users
- Paid API access only
