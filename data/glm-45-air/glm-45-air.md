## Overview

GLM-4.5-Air is an open-source Mixture-of-Experts language model released by Zhipu AI (知谱 AI) in July 2025. It complements the larger GLM-4.5 (355B) model as a lighter, more efficient variant.

## Architecture

- **Type**: Mixture-of-Experts (MoE)
- **Total Parameters**: 106 billion
- **Active Parameters per Query**: A fraction of total parameters (MoE routing)
- **License**: Open-source

## Capabilities

- Bilingual (Chinese/English) text generation and understanding
- Code generation and reasoning
- Instruction following
- Efficient inference due to sparse activation of expert sub-networks

## Release

- Announced: July 2025
- Released alongside GLM-4.5 (355B) as part of Zhipu AI's expanding GLM family
- Made available for download on Hugging Face and Zhipu AI's open model repositories

## Context

Zhipu AI, a spinout from Tsinghua University, has a long history of open-source Chinese language models dating back to the original GLM-6B and ChatGLM series. GLM-4.5-Air represents the company's continued commitment to open-source AI at scale.
