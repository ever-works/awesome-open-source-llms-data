## Overview

BlueLM is an open-source large language model developed by Vivo, a leading Chinese consumer electronics manufacturer. It represents Vivo's entry into foundational AI model development, contributing to the broader Chinese open-source LLM landscape.

## Capabilities

- Bilingual Chinese-English language understanding and generation
- General-purpose text generation and reasoning
- Instruction following and conversational AI
- Integration potential with Vivo's consumer device ecosystem

## Architecture

- Transformer-based language model
- Open weights published on GitHub and Hugging Face
- Licensed under permissive open-source terms

## Context

BlueLM is part of the broader trend of Chinese technology companies — beyond traditional tech firms — releasing open-source language models. Alongside models from other device manufacturers like Xiaomi (MiMo), it represents cross-industry AI investment in China.
