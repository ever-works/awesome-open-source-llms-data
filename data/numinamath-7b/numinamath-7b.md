## Overview

NuminaMath is a series of specialized language models trained to solve high-level mathematics problems using tool-integrated reasoning (TIR). The 7B model won the first progress prize of the AI Math Olympiad (AIMO).

## Competition Results

- **AIMO Progress Prize**: Won with 29/50 score
- **Public Test Set**: High performance
- **Private Test Set**: Verified results
- **Achievement**: First award-winning open mathematical reasoning model

## Model Variants

### NuminaMath-7B-TIR
- **Focus**: Tool-integrated reasoning
- **Performance**: AIMO winner
- **Scope**: Competition-level mathematics

### NuminaMath-7B-CoT
- **Focus**: Chain of Thought reasoning
- **Performance**: Competitive alternative
- **Scope**: Diverse math problems

## Training Methodology

**Stage 1: Broad Math Training**
- Natural language math problems
- Solution patterns
- Chain of Thought (CoT)
- Diverse problem types

**Stage 2: Tool-Integrated Reasoning**
- Synthetic dataset creation
- Decomposition into rationales
- Python program generation
- Output verification
- Executable solutions

## Problem Capability Levels

- **Strong**: AMC 12 level problems
- **Moderate**: AIME level problems
- **Weak**: Math Olympiad level problems
- **Challenge**: Geometry problems (limited)

## Limitations

- Optimized for competition mathematics only
- Not suitable for general chat
- Limited multimodal capabilities
- Struggles with geometry (no vision)
- Struggles on hardest problems

## Use Cases

- Mathematical problem solving
- Competition preparation
- Algorithm development
- Research in mathematical reasoning
- Educational applications
- Curriculum development
