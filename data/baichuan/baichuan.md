## Overview

Baichuan is an open-source LLM series from Baichuan Intelligence, one of China's leading AI startups. The family has evolved from initial 7B and 13B models to the latest Baichuan 4, which positions itself as the premier Chinese open-source LLM for domain-specific applications.

## Model Evolution

### Baichuan 1 (2023)
- **Baichuan-7B**: Initial 7 billion parameter model
- **Baichuan-13B**: Enhanced 13 billion parameter variant

### Baichuan 2
- Improved architecture and training
- Enhanced multilingual capabilities
- Better instruction-following

### Baichuan 4 (Latest)
Premier model with strong focus on specialized domains:
- Law
- Finance
- Medicine
- Classical Chinese literature

## Domain Specialization

Baichuan 4 has been specifically designed and trained for:

**Legal Domain**:
- Legal document analysis
- Case law understanding
- Legal reasoning

**Financial Domain**:
- Financial report analysis
- Market sentiment understanding
- Investment research support

**Medical Domain**:
- Medical literature understanding
- Clinical note analysis
- Medical terminology expertise

**Classical Chinese**:
- Ancient text interpretation
- Classical literature understanding
- Historical document analysis
