## Overview

llama.cpp is a plain C/C++ implementation for running large language models efficiently on consumer hardware, including CPU-only systems. It is one of the most performant and widely supported inference engines, particularly for GGUF quantized models.

## Features

- CPU-friendly inference, works on older hardware
- Supports GGUF quantized models
- Runs models without requiring a GPU
- Minimal dependencies
- Cross-platform support (Linux, macOS, Windows)
- Strong community and active development

## Deployment

- Designed for local, on-device inference
- Works on systems without dedicated GPUs
- Supports GGUF quantization formats for maximum compatibility
- Ideal when GPU resources are unavailable or cost-prohibitive
