## Overview

Pixtral 12B is Mistral AI's first multimodal model, released in 2024, combining advanced vision and language understanding capabilities. With 12 billion parameters, Pixtral significantly outperforms other open-source multimodal models, demonstrating Mistral's expertise extends beyond text-only models to vision-language tasks.

## Architecture

- **Parameters:** 12 billion
- **Modalities:** Vision and Language
- **Vision Encoder:** Advanced image understanding
- **Language Model:** Based on Mistral architecture
- **Design:** Integrated vision-language processing
