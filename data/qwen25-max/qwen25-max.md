## Overview

Qwen2.5-Max has redefined the open-weights landscape with parameter scale exceeding 1 trillion via Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture. It represents Alibaba Cloud's most advanced open-source language model.

## Architecture

- **Total Parameters**: 1+ trillion (via MoE)
- **Architecture**: Mixture-of-Experts
- **Language Support**: 119 languages
- **Model Sizes**: 4B to 235B parameters across the Qwen family

## Performance

**Mathematical Reasoning**:
- AIME25: 92.3% accuracy

**Coding Performance**:
- LiveCodeBench v6: 74.1%
- Qwen3-Coder: 70.6% on SWE-Bench Verified

**General Capabilities**:
- Strong multilingual understanding across 119 languages
- Excellent instruction-following
- Advanced reasoning capabilities

## Qwen Family Models

The Qwen ecosystem includes:
- **Qwen2.5-Max**: Flagship trillion-parameter model
- **Qwen3-Next**: Next generation foundation model
- **Qwen3-Omni**: Multimodal variant
- **Qwen3-Coder**: Specialized coding model
- Models ranging from 4B to 235B parameters
