## Overview

DoctorGLM is an open-source medical language model developed by ShanghaiTech University. It is built upon the ChatGLM family and fine-tuned on Chinese medical domain corpora for healthcare applications.

## Capabilities

- Medical consultation and symptom analysis
- Clinical decision support
- Medical knowledge retrieval and reasoning
- Patient interaction and triage assistance
- Chinese medical terminology understanding

## Architecture

- Fine-tuned variant based on ChatGLM foundation model
- Domain-specific training on Chinese medical literature and clinical data
- Open weights released publicly

## Use Cases

- Healthcare providers seeking AI-assisted clinical support
- Medical education and training
- Patient-facing health information systems
- Medical research and knowledge management

## Deployment

- Open weights available for download and local deployment
- Suitable for privacy-sensitive healthcare environments
- Part of a growing ecosystem of domain-specific Chinese LLMs
