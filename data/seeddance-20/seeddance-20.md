## Overview

SeedDance 2.0 is ByteDance's next-generation video synthesis model, released in February 2026 alongside Doubao 2.0 (LLM) and Seedream 5.0 (image generation). It completes ByteDance's trifecta of a coordinated multimodal AI suite spanning text, image, and video.

## Features

- Next-generation video synthesis for content creation
- Optimized for social media and marketing applications
- Integrated with ByteDance's content ecosystem
- Coordination with text (Doubao 2.0) and image (Seedream 5.0) generation

## Ecosystem Position

SeedDance 2.0 is part of ByteDance's unified multimodal AI strategy. The ability to generate coordinated text, image, and video content from a single AI stack could significantly streamline content production workflows for businesses in content marketing and social media.

## Availability

- Access through ByteDance's Doubao ecosystem and Volcano Engine platform
- API-based access rather than open-weight release
- Part of the integrated Doubao multimodal suite
