## Overview

ShieldGemma is a specialized safety evaluation model released by Google on July 31, 2024. Built on Gemma 2, it provides content moderation and safety evaluation capabilities for text and images.

## Primary Functions

- User input safety evaluation
- Model output safety assessment
- Content moderation
- Harm detection and classification
- Safety compliance checking

## Harm Categories

ShieldGemma targets evaluation across four primary harm categories:
1. Category 1: Illegal and dangerous content
2. Category 2: Hateful and harmful content
3. Category 3: Sexually explicit content
4. Category 4: Misinformation and manipulation

## Architecture

- Based on: Gemma 2 foundation
- Training: Instruction-tuning for safety tasks
- Input: Text and images
- Output: Safety assessments and classifications
