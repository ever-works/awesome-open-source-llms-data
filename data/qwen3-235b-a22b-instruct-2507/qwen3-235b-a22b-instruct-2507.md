## Overview
Qwen3-235B-A22B-Instruct-2507 is the flagship model in Alibaba's Qwen series, known for its consistent contributions to the open-source LLM ecosystem. This model pushes the boundaries of performance in various essential domains.

## Technical Specifications
- **Total Parameters:** 235 Billion
- **Active Parameters per Token:** 22 Billion across 128 experts, utilizing an Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture.
- **Multilingual Support:** Supports over 100 languages and dialects, offering stronger multilingual coverage than previous Qwen iterations.
- **Context Length:** Natively supports 262,144 tokens, and can be extended to over 1 million tokens with appropriate hardware.

## Performance
- **Instruction Following, Coding, Math, and Science Benchmarks:** Delivers state-of-the-art performance.
- **Outperforms:** Demonstrates superior performance compared to GPT-4o and DeepSeek-V3 on benchmarks such as GPQA, AIME25, and LiveCodeBench.

## Distinctive Features
- **Ultra-Long Context:** Its exceptional context length makes it an ideal choice for RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) systems, extensive agent traces, and processing large documents.

## Related Models
- **Qwen3-Next-80B-A3B:** A more efficient follow-on model that matches the 235B variant in many benchmarks, with particular advantages in ultra-long-context tasks.

## Best For
Multilingual applications, RAG pipelines, long-context document analysis, and agentic systems requiring deep context.
