## Overview

gpt-oss-120b marks OpenAI's first fully open-weight large language model release since GPT-2. This 120-billion-parameter model represents a significant shift in OpenAI's approach, bringing open-source capabilities that match or exceed some of their proprietary offerings.

## Architecture

- **Parameters:** 120 billion
- **Model Type:** GPT architecture (decoder-only Transformer)
- **Release Significance:** First open-weight model from OpenAI in years
- **Training:** Leverages OpenAI's advanced training techniques

## Key Features

- Full open-weight access (not just API)
- Competitive with OpenAI's proprietary models
- Advanced reasoning capabilities
- Strong performance across diverse tasks
- Production-quality model from leading AI lab
- Can be self-hosted and fine-tuned

## Performance Highlights

### Benchmark Comparison
Matches or surpasses **o4-mini** on core benchmarks:
- **AIME:** Competitive mathematical reasoning
- **MMLU:** Strong general knowledge and reasoning
- **TauBench:** Advanced task understanding
- **HealthBench:** Medical and health domain performance

### Comparison with OpenAI Models
- Outperforms proprietary models like OpenAI o1 and GPT-4o on certain benchmarks
- Demonstrates that open-weight models can compete with closed alternatives
- Represents OpenAI's commitment to open research

## Significance

### For the Open-Source Community
- Validates open-source approach with top-tier performance
