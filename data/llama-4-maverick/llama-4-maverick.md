## Overview
Llama 4 Maverick is an open-source large language model known for its exceptionally long context window.

## Features
- **Context Window:** 1M tokens (joint-longest on the board).
- **Parameters:** 400B.

## Best for
- Tasks requiring extensive long-context understanding.

## Benchmarks
- MMLU: 85.5
- MMLU-Pro: 80.5
- HumanEval: 62.0
- LiveCodeBench: 43.4
- GPQA Diamond: 69.8
- Chatbot Arena: 1328
