## Overview

Llama 4 Maverick is Meta's flagship multimodal model, representing the high-end of the Llama 4 family. With 400B parameters, it delivers exceptional performance across text and image understanding tasks.

## Model Architecture

- **Total Parameters**: 400 billion
- **Architecture**: Mixture-of-Experts (MoE)
- **Modality**: Native multimodal (text + image)
- **Pre-training**: 22 trillion tokens of multimodal data
- **Design**: Optimized for frontier performance

## Training Data

- Comprehensive multimodal dataset
- 22 trillion tokens of training data
- Public, licensed, and proprietary sources
- Meta product and service information
- High-quality curation for performance
