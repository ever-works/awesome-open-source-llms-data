## Overview

Yi 1.5 is an open-source large language model developed by 01.AI, a Chinese AI startup founded by Kai-Fu Lee. The model is particularly known for its ability to handle long sequences and maintain contextual coherence, earning recognition as a "dark horse" in the open-source LLM landscape.

## Architecture

- **Model Type:** Transformer-based language model
- **Context Handling:** Optimized for long-sequence processing
- **Training:** Advanced training on diverse datasets
- **Optimization:** Designed for efficient long-context understanding

## Key Features

- Exceptional long-sequence handling
- Superior contextual coherence across extended texts
- Strong coding and programming capabilities
- Good general-purpose performance
- Efficient architecture for deployment
- Multilingual support with emphasis on Chinese and English
